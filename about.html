About Me

Hello! I'm Md Parvej Hossain, a Web Developer at Hi-Care Group with a passion for technology, problem-solving, and sustainable innovation.

I specialize in supporting complex software environments and delivering efficient technical solutions. My current role at Hi-Care Group allows me to leverage my expertise in Web support.

I hold a BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from the Green University of Bangladesh, where I have also as the Member of the Green University Computer Club (GUCC).

My research interests focus on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Analysis and the Research work in Heart Disease Prediction. I'm passionate about applying machine learning to heart disease prediction, using my skills to drive impactful healthcare improvements.

I'm driven by a commitment to continuous learning and the application of cutting-edge technology to tackle real-world challenges. My goal is to make meaningful contributions within the dynamic fields of technology and innovation.
